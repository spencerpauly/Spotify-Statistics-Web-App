About Me

My name is Spencer Pauly. I’m a college sophomore studying Computer Science at University of Minnesota Duluth. I was first introduced to the STEM fields back when I was 11 and was given a lego Mindstorm to play with. These lego robots literally changed my life, as I would play with them for HOURS each day. I built some really awesome projects, and I think getting such an early introduction to the STEM fields was by far the most pivotal moment of my life. I had the perfect storm of youthful creativity, free time, and easy-to-use building blocks which enabled me to literally play god when I built these robots. It was amazing. Since then I’ve moved on to many different things. I used to run game servers for video games where I was the sole administrator for over 80 concurrent users back when I was 13, then moved to web design where I designed numerous personal sites using bootstrap and other web technologies. Once I got to college I was then able to begin expanding my toolbelt, and since then I have made a working neural network from scratch and done various other projects for my CS coursework. In the future, I hope to retain my childhood creativity with the hopes of bringing it to market someday through starting some sort of business. For the short term, however, I am much more focused on acquiring skills so that when that time comes I have nothing stopping me except my own willpower. In conclusion, if you want to get an even deeper look into my headspace, please continue reading the rest of my website. I will occasionally post blog posts on a variety of subjects, and keep this site updated with my most recent projects. Thank you.
